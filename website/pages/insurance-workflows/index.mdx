# Module 2: Guided Workflows

> **Time:** ~90 minutes total | **Prerequisites:** [Module 1: Fundamentals](/fundamentals/first-steps)

In Module 1 you learned the basics: how to give Codex instructions, reference files, and set up project memory. Now you will apply those skills to **real insurance work**.

Each tutorial walks you through a complete workflow step by step, with sample prompts, expected outputs, and coaching tips. You are never left guessing -- we show you what to type, what to look for in the output, and how to refine the results.

---

## The Three Workflows

| Tutorial | Duration | What You Will Do |
|----------|----------|------------------|
| [2.1: Underwriting Brief](/insurance-workflows/underwriting-brief) | 30 min | Analyze a commercial property portfolio and generate a structured underwriting brief for senior review |
| [2.2: Loss Ratio Analysis](/insurance-workflows/loss-ratio-analysis) | 30 min | Calculate motor portfolio loss ratios by region, create visualizations, and produce an executive summary |
| [2.3: Market Assessment](/insurance-workflows/market-assessment) | 30 min | Use OpenAI Codex's web search to research the Portuguese market and build a strategy brief |

Each tutorial introduces new techniques:

- **2.1** -- Structured data input, analytical prompting, iterative document refinement
- **2.2** -- Working with data files, generating visualizations, quantitative analysis
- **2.3** -- Web search integration, scoring frameworks, board-level document generation

---

## How This Differs from Practice Exercises

These guided workflows are **tutorials** -- we walk through every step together. The [Practice Exercises](/exercises) are **independent challenges** where you get a business problem and data files, and work through them on your own. Think of Module 2 as the driving lesson; the exercises are the road test.
