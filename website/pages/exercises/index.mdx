# Practice Exercises

These are **independent, hands-on challenges** designed for the live workshop session. Each exercise gives you a business problem, a persona, and a set of data files -- you figure out how to solve it with OpenAI Codex.

Unlike the [Guided Workflows](/insurance-workflows) in Module 2, these exercises do not walk you through every prompt. They tell you what to achieve and give you tips, but the approach is yours to decide. This is where you put what you have learned into practice.

---

## The Exercises

| Exercise | Persona | Duration | What You Build |
|----------|---------|----------|----------------|
| [0: Executive Onramp (Zero to One)](/exercises/executive-onramp) | Executives | 90 min | Zero-to-one executive prompting workflow with copy/paste wins and progressive challenge |
| [1: Data Insight Tool](/exercises/data-insight-tool) | Isabel Santos (Strategy) | 40 min | Market white space analysis across 68 regions with interactive charts and an executive memo |
| [2: Claims Triage](/exercises/claims-triage) | Ana Martinez (Claims) | 45 min | Classification, prioritization, and routing system for 220 incoming claims |
| [3: Automated Reporting](/exercises/automated-reporting) | Pierre Dupont (Actuarial) | 55 min | Quarterly claims report with visualizations and a reusable auto-update script |

Each exercise includes its own data files in the `exercises/` directory. See the [Data Catalog](/exercises/data-catalog) for a full reference of every file, column, and join key.

---

## How to Start

1. Open your terminal in the exercise directory (e.g., `exercises/exercise-0-executive-onramp/`)
2. Start OpenAI Codex with `codex`
3. Follow the instructions in the exercise page

---

## Tips

- **Start by exploring the data.** Ask Codex to read and summarize the CSV files before jumping into analysis.
- **Be specific about output format.** If you want a table, chart, or memo, say so explicitly.
- **Iterate.** The first output is rarely the final one. Refine with follow-up prompts.
- **If you get stuck**, check the troubleshooting section at the bottom of each exercise page.
